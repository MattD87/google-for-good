GOOGLE ADS AND GOOGLE AD GRANTS

Google Ads is an online advertising platform developed by Google. You, as an advertiser, can display brief advertisements, service offerings, product listings, video content, and ask web users for donations within the Google ad network.
‍
Google Ad Grants is Google’s unique program that provides an opportunity for nonprofits to have free access to Google business tools. Google supports nonprofits with $10,000 per month in free advertising on your Google Ads platform.

Google Ad Grants allows you to set up unlimited ad campaigns in your Google Ad Grants account. Google will show your ads on the search engine results page when people search for specific terms and keywords.
